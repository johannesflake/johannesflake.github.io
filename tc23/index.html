Tensor categories in representation theory

Instructors Prof. Dr. Catharina Stroppel and Dr. Johannes Flake
Lectures Wednesdays and Fridays, 10–12 am, R1.008
Prerequisites Algebra I & II, Representation Theory I, basic knowledge in category theory
Material t.b.a.

Course content
  • additive and abelian categories
  • monoidal and braided categories
  • tensor categories
  • Hopf algebras
  • Tannakian reconstruction
  • Deligne's theorem on tensor categories
